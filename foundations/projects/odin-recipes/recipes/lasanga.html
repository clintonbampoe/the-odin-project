Chicken and Spinach Alfredo Lasanga Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package lasanga noodles
  • 3 cups heavy cream
  • 2 (10.8 ounce) cans condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 5 mushrooms, diced
  • 1 cooked rotisserie chicken, shredded
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 bunch fresh spinach, rinsed
  • 3 cups shredded mozarella cheese

Directions

  1. Step 1
    2. Gather the Ingredients.
  2. Step 2
    3. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Bring a large poot of lightly salted water to boil. Add lasanga noodles and cook until al dente, about 8 to 10 minutes. Drain, and rinse with cold water.
  3. Step 3
    4. Mix heavy cream, cream of mushroom soup, Parmesan cheese, and butter in a saucepan over low heat; simmer, stirring frequently, until well blended.
  4. Step 4
    5. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir onion in olive oil until tender, then add garlic and mushrooms.
  5. Step 5
    6. Mix in chicken, and cook until heated through. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Step 6
    7. Lightly coat the bottom of a 9x13-inch with enought of the cream sauce mixture to coat.
  7. Step 7
    8. Layer with 1/3 of the lasanga noodles, 1/2 cup ricotta, 1/2 of the spinach.
  8. Step 8
    9. Add 1/2 the chicken mixture, and 1 cup mozarella. Top with 1/3 the cream sauce mixture, and repeat the layers.
  9. Step 9
    10. Place the remaining noodles on top, and spread with remaining sauce.
  10. Step 10
    11. Bake 1 hour in the preheated oven, or until brown and bubbly. Top with the remaining mozarella, and contine baking until cheese is melted and lightly browned.
